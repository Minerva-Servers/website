About Me

Hello there, my name is Riggs! I used to go under some old aliases such as "Zombie boi" or "Y.E.E.T." back before 2019. I used to play a lot of Half-Life 2 Roleplay back in 2015 and 2017, coming soo faar I decided to open my own community called Minerva Servers.

I have been developing for Garry's Mod since 2020 and have been a part of the Garry's Mod community for over 12 years. I have a huge passion for creating maps in the Source Engine, some of my creations do tend to go into the trash bin.

Like I said, I'm a community representative for my own community, Minerva Servers. I'm also a developer for the community, creating custom content for our servers. I'm always looking for new opportunities to grow and expand my skills, so feel free to reach out if you have any questions or opportunities for me!

Projects

Minerva Servers

Founded on the 12th of October, 2022, Minerva Servers is an online Garry's Mod community that hosts a wide range of servers for its dedicated player base. It's dedicated players once being just friends of mine, now have grown to be a large community of players all around Garry's Mod and other games.

It's initial focus was to be a faithful SCP Containment Breach Roleplaying Server within Garry's Mod, but later expanded to include Half-Life 2 Roleplay, which has since become its core feature.

Over time, it has expanded significantly and become a prominent and well-respected name in the Garry's Mod community. The community continues to grow, offering various servers and activities to engage and entertain its members.

    Helix Framework Plugins

    This repository contains a collection of public plugins I've created over the years for the Helix Framework, a roleplaying framework for Garry's Mod.

    VAC (Vanguard Anti-Cheat)

    Vanguard Anti-Cheat (VAC) is a simple anti-cheat system for Garry's Mod, taking inspiration from the Impulse Anti-Cheat (IAC) system. This project is meant to be used for private purposes and is not intended for commercial use.

    VWEP (Vanguard Weapon Base)

    Vanguard Weapon Base (VWEP) is a simple weapon base for Garry's Mod designed to be easy to use and expand upon.
