Rebirth of Half-Life 2 Roleplay Retail

With the realisation that the impulse and Helix combination was not working out, we have decided to rebirth our Half-Life 2 Roleplay server with the retail look and feel in mind. This means that we will be using the default Half-Life 2 map and assets, and we will be making use of the Helix framework to allow for a more efficient development process.

Our goal is to provide a more authentic Half-Life 2 Roleplay experience for our players, and we believe that this change will help us achieve that. We are excited to see how this new direction will impact our community, and we hope that our players will enjoy the changes that we have made.

If you have any questions or feedback about this new direction, please feel free to reach out to us on our Discord server. We are always looking for ways to improve our servers and provide the best possible experience for our players, so your input is greatly appreciated.

Thank you for your continued support, and we look forward to seeing you in our discord!
